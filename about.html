About Us

Kakxy|Store is a shoe care company that provides a range of services to keep your shoes looking brand new.

Our History

We started out as a small family business in 1995, providing shoe cleaning and repair services in our local community. As our reputation grew, so did our business, and we now offer a wide range of services to customers around the world.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide the highest quality shoe care services to our customers, using the latest technology and techniques to ensure that your shoes stay looking brand new for as long as possible.

Our Team

Our team is made up of highly skilled and experienced professionals who are dedicated to providing the best possible service to our customers. We take great pride in our work, and are committed to ensuring that your shoes are always in the best possible condition.
